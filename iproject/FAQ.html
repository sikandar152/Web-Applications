FAQ/HELP


How is this site helpful to institutes?
        This site is a medium by which institutes can conduct online exams.
        They can register on the site and then can enter the exam details and questions.The institutes also provide a list of students that are eligible for the exam click here

How one(institutes) can register on this site ?
        Instiitues can register by clicking on the link on the home page or just click here
        After that fill all the details very carefully.

How students can login a nd give their exams ?
        Students can login by clicking on the link given on home page or just click here
        Students need to remember the ID provided to them by the institutes. After login students can take up their exams and can see their results then and there

What if a institute wants to edit questions already entered?
        Instiitues can easily edit or delete the questions through the options given to him once it login.
        Institutes can login by clicking on the link given on home page or just click here

What if a institute wants to view the list of students or make some changes to it?
        Instiitues can easily view or edit the students list through the options given to him once it login.
        Institutes can login by clicking on the link given on home page or just click here

Can insttiutes set their own time limit for exam?
        Instiitues can give the time limit for exam once they register.
        They can even change it by logging in at a later time.

Is there any provision for negative marking?
        Instiitues can enter both positive and negative marks for correct or incorrect question respectively once they register.
        They can even change it by logging in at a later time click here
